NETRA-AI

"Saving sight through smart code." Netra-AI is a diagnostic tool designed to bring expert-level retinal screening to rural India, helping doctors identify diabetic blindness using transparent, explainable AI.

A Crisis of Care

India is the "Diabetes Capital" with over 100 million patients. However, we have a massive shortage of specialists—often only one expert for every 60,000 patients.

The challenge is creating a system robust enough to handle low-quality images from affordable, portable cameras used in remote health camps.

The Trust Gap

  • Confidence: Doctors often hesitate to use AI "black boxes." We make our AI transparent so they can verify the findings instantly.
  • Precision: Tiny red spots (lesions) are easily missed by tired human eyes. Our AI acts as a 24/7 assistant that never misses a detail.
Our AI Pipeline

From Image to Diagnosis

1. Smart Cleaning

We use Ben Graham’s Method to normalize lighting and shadows. This ensures that even a photo taken in a dark rural camp looks clear and consistent for the AI.

2. Deep Analysis

Our EfficientNet-B3 brain analyzes the retina's condition across 5 stages of severity. It's optimized to detect microscopic blood leaks that signal early-stage disease.

3. Visual Proof

Using Grad-CAM, the AI generates heatmaps that literally "highlight" the damaged areas, showing the doctor exactly where the problem lies.

Industry-Leading Performance

Kappa Score: 0.8985

This isn't just a high score—it’s "Near-Perfect Agreement." While standard models like VGG16 struggle with medical nuances, Netra-AI matches the decision-making of world-class vitreoretinal specialists.
